Core Concept: Prime Numbers
Bengali Explanation: আমরা জানি যে, ১-এর চেয়ে বড় যে সব সংখ্যার ১ ও সংখ্যাটি ছাড়া অন্য কোনো গুণনীয়ক নেই, সেগুলো মৌলিক সংখ্যা। ইরাটোস্থিনিস (Eratosthenes) ছাঁকনির সাহায্যে সহজেই মৌলিক সংখ্যা নির্ণয় করা যায়। ১ থেকে ১০০ পর্যন্ত স্বাভাবিক সংখ্যাগুলো একটি চার্টে লিখি। এবার সবচেয়ে ছোট মৌলিক সংখ্যা ২ চিহ্নিত করি এবং এর গুণিতকগুলো কেটে দেই। এরপর ক্রমান্বয়ে ৩, ৫ এবং ৭ ইত্যাদি মৌলিক সংখ্যার গুণিতকগুলো কেটে দিই। তালিকায় যে সংখ্যাগুলো টিকে রইল সেগুলো মৌলিক সংখ্যা।
English Translation: We know that prime numbers are those numbers greater than 1 that have no other factors except 1 and the number itself. Prime numbers can be easily found using the Sieve of Eratosthenes. Write the natural numbers from 1 to 100 in a chart. Now, identify the smallest prime number, 2, and cross out all its multiples. Then, successively cross out the multiples of 3, 5, 7, and so on. The numbers that remain in the list are the prime numbers.
Mathematical Notation: A prime number p is a natural number greater than 1 that has no positive divisors other than 1 and p.
Visual Support: The text describes a table of numbers from 1 to 100 with multiples of prime numbers crossed out.
Real-World Connection: Prime numbers are fundamental in cryptography and computer science.

PROBLEM-SOLVING FRAMEWORK
EXAMPLE STRUCTURE
Problem Statement:
- Bengali Version: সংখ্যাগুলোর পরবর্তী দুইটি সংখ্যা নির্ণয় কর: ৩, ১০, ১৭, ২৪, ৩১, ...
- English Translation: Find the next two numbers in the sequence: 3, 10, 17, 24, 31, ...
- Given Information: The sequence 3, 10, 17, 24, 31, ...
- Unknown Elements: The next two numbers in the sequence.

Solution Path:
1. Understanding Phase
   - Key information identification: The given sequence of numbers.
   - Relevant formula selection: Identifying the pattern of differences between consecutive terms.
   - Strategy planning: Calculate the difference between consecutive terms to find the pattern.

2. Solution Steps
   - Step-by-step breakdown:
     - Calculate the difference between 10 and 3: 10 - 3 = 7
     - Calculate the difference between 17 and 10: 17 - 10 = 7
     - Calculate the difference between 24 and 17: 24 - 17 = 7
     - Calculate the difference between 31 and 24: 31 - 24 = 7
     - The difference between consecutive terms is constant, which is 7.
     - To find the next number, add 7 to the last number: 31 + 7 = 38
     - To find the number after that, add 7 to 38: 38 + 7 = 45
   - Mathematical reasoning: The sequence is an arithmetic progression with a common difference of 7.
   - Common mistake warnings: Not recognizing the constant difference.
   - Visual aids used: None.

3. Answer Verification
   - Method of checking: Verify that the difference between the new terms and the previous terms is also 7.
   - Alternative approaches: None.
   - Units and precision: The numbers are integers.

- Unknown Elements: Sum of squares for 29 and 37, the next 4 numbers, the sum of the first 50 numbers.

   - Relevant formula selection: Identifying the pattern of increase, sum of arithmetic series.

     - 37 = 1^2 + 6^2

   - Common mistake warnings: Incorrect sum of squares, miscalculating the next terms, incorrect sum formula.

   - Method of checking: Verify the sum of squares, check the next terms, use the sum formula.
